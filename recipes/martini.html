Sugar Cookie Martini

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla icing, or as needed
  • 1 teaspoon sprinkles, or as needed
  • 1 cup ice, or as needed
  • 3 fluid ounces rum cream liqueur (such as RumChataÂ®)
  • 1 1/2 ounces vanilla vodka
  • 1/4 ounce Amaretto

Steps

  1. Rim martini glass with icing and sprinkles; set aside.
  2. Fill a cocktail shaker with ice; pour in rum cream, vodka, and amaretto. Cover and shake until the outside of shaker has frosted. Strain into a chilled glass.
